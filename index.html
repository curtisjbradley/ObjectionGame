Q - Proper question (No Objection)
A - Argumentative
B - Best Evidence Rule - (Asking for hearsay rather than referencing a report)
C - Calls for a conclusion of ultimate fact
F - Lack of foundation
H - Hearsay
I - Irrelevant
L - Leading
M - Compound question
P - Violates a privilege. (Attorney-Client, Doctor-Patient, etc.)
S - Speculation
V - Vague or Ambiguous
